Our Beliefs

What we believe about God, the Bible, etc.

THE GODHEAD: We believe that God is the one, true and living God existing and manifesting Himself eternally as three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

JESUS CHRIST: We believe that Jesus Christ was begotten by the Holy Spirit, was born of the Virgin Mary, died as the sinner's substitute, making perfect redemption for the sins of the world, was raised bodily from the dead, ascended to the right: hand of the Father in heaven where He is the believer's High Priest and Advocate. (Matthew 1:20; Luke 1:35)

HOLY SPIRIT: We believe that the Holy Spirit is equal in every attribute of deity with God the Father and with God the Son, that He is now resident in the hearts of believers, that He performs the miracle of the new birth in the hearts of those receiving Christ as Saviour. (I Corinthians 12:13)

THE BIBLE: We believe that the Bible, consisting of sixty-six (66) books, is the full and complete revelation of God's will for man, the only infallible and final authority in all matters of faith and life. (II Timothy 3:15-17)

MAN: We believe that all men are sinners and apart from Christ they are presently and eternally dead in their sins, separated from God and under His condemnation. (John 3:18, 36)

SALVATION: We believe that all who receive by faith the Lord Jesus Christ as personal Saviour are justified on the ground of His shed blood, are born again by the Holy Spirit through the Word, thereby becoming the children of God, and are indwelt, sealed by the Holy Spirit until the day of redemption. (Romans 3:24-25)

RESURRECTION: We believe in the personal visible, premillenial return of the Lord Jesus Christ. (Acts 1:11; I John 3:2)

SATAN: We believe that Satan is a person and that Hell is a place of eternal punishment for Satan and all the unsaved.
